Lasagna

Description

Making Lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below.

This Lasagna is one of the most favorites recipes thanks to it's high degree of customization. Still, the original ingredients will provide you with a delicious dish.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • 1 Onion
  • Two cloves of garlic
  • 1 Can of crushed tomato
  • Two tablespoons of white sugar
  • Spices and seasoning (we recommend fresh parsley,dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and black pepper)
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses (We recommend parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta cheese)
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers. Use Parmesan for the top
